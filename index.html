How It Works

From zero to speaking in 4 simple steps

No complicated setup. No textbooks. Just start talking.

01

Pick a language

Choose from 10 languages — Spanish, English, German, French, Italian, and more. Set your CEFR level from A1 (beginner) to C2 (native).

02

Choose a scenario

At a cafe, job interview, asking directions — or just go free-form. Your tutor adapts to any topic.

03

Talk naturally

Speak with your AI tutor using voice or text. Get response suggestions when you're stuck. No judgment, just practice.

04

Review & grow

See every mistake color-coded with corrections. Save new words to flashcards. Watch your progress on the dashboard.
